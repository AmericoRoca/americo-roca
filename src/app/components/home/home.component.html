About me

Hi! I´m Américo Roca, I´m originally from Madrid (Spain).
Since I was little I have always liked the world of computing, the internet was a great discovery for me since I was fascinated by the websites I used to navigate back then, such as Wikipedia among many others to help me with my homework.
During the high school recess, I sneaked into the computer room where I made my first website, with HTML, a fundamental web page since at that time neither the technology was that developed, nor was I that well informed.

I grew up and reached adolescence... what a wrong time, right?

Let's just say that back then I was more interested in hanging out with friends than studying, so I finished high school and went to work. After a long time working in different things, waiter, sales assistant, stocker, etc., where I have gained a lot of experience in dealing with customers.
I continued with my concern for the creation of applications and websites, that year many things changed in my life and I moved to Amsterdam (Holland).
That year I started to take up creating web pages again and started studying HTML, CSS, and javascript at "FreeCodeCamp" where I learned the basics of creating web pages.

Since I was always working, I didn't have much time to dedicate to it, but that year 2020... changed everyone's life, including mine. I lost my job, due to the pandemic... but you always have to look on the positive side of things, now I had time to study, so I signed up for the Higher Technician course in web development at the Universitat Oberta de Catalunya, which would last two years. In this course, I have learned the fundamentals of programming, object-oriented programming, design, and creation of databases and many programming languages, which has allowed me to have a wide knowledge of both the frontend and the backend.
